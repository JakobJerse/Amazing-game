Amazing Game

The goal of the game is to find a way out of the maze. To get out, you must first find the key that opens the door to freedom. Follow the ringing of the phone - maybe someone will tell you where the key is hidden. Beware of killer spirits and deadly traps.

Controls:

  • W - move forward
  • A - move left
  • S - move back
  • D - move right
  • E - answer the phone
